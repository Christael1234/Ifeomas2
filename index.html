Who I Am

Behind every great creator is a great producer.

Fashion Beauty Lifestyle Tech Real Estate NGO

I'm Ifeoma — a Nigerian content producer who works exclusively with personal brands in fashion, beauty and lifestyle. I handle the full creative pipeline: the ideas, the scripts, the edits, the graphics, the planning. Everything that makes your content feel intentional and premium.

I've worked across tech, real estate, justice-focused nonprofits, and personal brands — which means I understand how to adapt creative thinking to different worlds. But fashion, beauty and lifestyle is where I come alive. It's where storytelling meets aesthetics, and that's exactly my language.

How It Works

Simple. Collaborative.
No stress on your end.

01
We Talk

A quick 15-minute call or DM conversation where I learn your brand, your goals, your aesthetic, and what's not working right now. No lengthy forms.

02
I Plan

I build out a content plan tailored to your world — ideas, scripts, a calendar. You review, we adjust. You're in control the whole time.

03
I Produce

Editing, design, voiceovers — all the production happens on my end. You get polished, ready-to-post content delivered on time, every time.

04
You Show Up

You post. Your audience sees the best version of your brand. We review what performed and refine for next month. It gets better every cycle.
